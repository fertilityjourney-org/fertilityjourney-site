Been through it?

Help someone who’s just starting

Whether your journey ended with a child, a different path, or a decision to stop, what you learned is worth a lot to someone at the beginning. Here is how to pass it on. These programs are new and we are building them with the first people who raise a hand.

Share your story

Real stories are the heart of “your experience is shared.” We publish stories only with your written consent, you choose how you are named, and you can withdraw it at any time.

Email us your story

Mentor someone

We are designing a peer-mentor program that pairs people early in their journey with someone who has lived it. If you want to be among the first mentors, tell us — we will shape the program with you.

Raise your hand

Give

We plan to become a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Until the IRS grants that status, we do not accept donations — from anyone. The most valuable things you can give today are your story and your time. When tax-deductible giving opens, we will say so here.
