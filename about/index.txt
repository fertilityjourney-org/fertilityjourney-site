d:Ta7a,<h1 id="about-fertilityjourney">About FertilityJourney</h1>
<p>Finding out that having a child might be hard is one of the loneliest moments a person can face. The information out there is a maze — sales pitches dressed as advice, forums full of guesses, numbers with no source. FertilityJourney exists to be the opposite of that: an independent guide through infertility, with real options, realistic timelines, true costs, and the plain fact that you are not the only one carrying this.</p>
<h2 id="what-we-do">What we do</h2>
<p>We help you understand where you are and what usually comes next. Not what you <em>should</em> do — that belongs to you and a doctor who knows your history — but what the paths actually look like: what a test tells you, what a treatment costs, how long things really take, and what questions are worth asking before you spend a dollar or a month.</p>
<h2 id="what-we-will-always-be">What we will always be</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Independent.</strong> Providers never pay to appear here, and nothing we publish is an advertisement. How we&#39;re funded, and the money we turn down, is spelled out on our <a href="/trust/">Trust &amp; Transparency</a> page.</li>
<li><strong>Sourced.</strong> Every medical figure we publish is cited to peer-reviewed research and national registry data — SART, CDC, ASRM, WHO — with the source and the date we last checked it shown on the page. We chose tested evidence over any one person&#39;s opinion. When a credentialed clinician has reviewed a page, we say so by name, and only then.</li>
<li><strong>Realistic.</strong> We publish honest timelines and true cost ranges, not best-case stories. Hope that isn&#39;t grounded in the real numbers isn&#39;t kind — it&#39;s just another way to waste someone&#39;s time and money.</li>
<li><strong>Outcome-neutral.</strong> Whether your path leads to treatment, a pause, another route to family, or a different life than you planned, we have no stake in your choice. We only want it to be an informed one.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="who-we-are">Who we are</h2>
<p>FertilityJourney is operated by <strong>Fertility Journey LLC</strong>, a Virginia company. We plan to become a <strong>501(c)(3) nonprofit</strong> — we&#39;ll say so here when that status is in place, and we don&#39;t accept donations until it is.</p>
<p>We are an educational resource. We are not a medical provider, we do not give medical advice, and we cannot diagnose anything. What we can do is make the road ahead a little less bewildering, and a lot less lonely.</p>
<p>Reach us any time: <strong><a href="mailto:contact@fertilityjourney.org">contact@fertilityjourney.org</a></strong>.</p>
